## Why claude-nexus?

I use Claude every day. As conversations pile up, the sidebar becomes a mess with no clean way to group chats or jump back to important parts of a long thread.

`claude-nexus` turns that friction into a smoother workflow with practical tools designed for daily Claude users.

## What you can do

- Organize chats into folders that make sense to you
- Navigate long conversations with a visual timeline
- Open utilities from a draggable floating ball
- Reuse prompts from a built-in prompt library
- Export chat history in Markdown or JSON
